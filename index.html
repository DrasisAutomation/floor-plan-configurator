Complete Automation Design Solution

From concept to installation-ready plans, DomoticsDesigner provides everything you need for professional automation system design.

Intuitive Floor Plan Design

Draw new floor plans or upload existing ones in multiple formats. Our drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to create accurate representations of any space.

Comprehensive Device Library

Access a vast library of smart switches, relays, speakers, sensors, and DB boxes. Drag and drop devices directly onto your floor plans.

Advanced Wiring Management

Design and visualize wiring layouts for KNX, IR cables, CAT6, neutral cables, and more. Calculate cable lengths and requirements automatically.

Multi-Brand Integration

Seamlessly integrate devices from leading automation brands. Our platform supports Lumi Electronics and dozens of other manufacturers.

Export & Documentation

Generate professional installation documents, wiring diagrams, bills of materials, and client presentations with a single click.

Shareable Design Files

Save your automation designs as project files that can be opened, edited, and updated by anyone you share them with—perfect for teams, clients, and collaborative workflows.

Integrated with Leading Brands

DomoticsDesigner supports devices and systems from top automation manufacturers worldwide.
