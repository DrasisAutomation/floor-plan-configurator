Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DomoticsDesigner.

What file formats can I import for floor plans?

DomoticsDesigner supports file formats including PNG, JPG and JPEG

Which automation brands are currently supported?

Our platform currently supports Lumi Electronics and is continuously expanding to include dozens of other manufacturers. You can suggest specific brands through our support portal.

How often is the device library updated?

Our device library is updated monthly with new products from supported brands. You can also request specific devices to be added through our support portal.

What types of wiring can I design with DomoticsDesigner?

You can design and visualize various wiring layouts including KNX, IR cables, CAT6, neutral cables, phase wires, and more. The platform automatically calculates cable lengths and requirements.

What documentation can I export from my designs?

You can generate professional installation documents, wiring diagrams, bills of materials, and client presentations with a single click. All exports are ready for sharing with clients and installers.

Can I collaborate with team members on projects?

Yes! DomoticsDesigner includes team collaboration features. You can invite team members, share design files, and work together on the same project. Saved designs can be opened, edited, and updated by anyone you share them with.

What devices are included in your library?

Our library includes smart switches, relays, speakers, sensors, wall mount displays, IR blasters, DB boxes, and all necessary wiring components. We're constantly adding new devices to support comprehensive automation system design.
